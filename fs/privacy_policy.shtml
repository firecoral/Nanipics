Privacy Policy

NaniPics has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and the protection of your information. The following explains the ways information will be gathered and the choices you can make about these practices.

Order Placement: On some of our Web pages you may order products. The types of information collected at these pages are name, address, contact and billing information, email address and credit card information. This information is used for completion of your order. Order information is also made available to the lab that produces the products.

The NaniPics Web site uses "cookies". Cookies are small files written on your computer that help us with your order. NaniPics uses the information to help complete a transaction or to communicate back to you. The cookie is automatically removed when your order is placed. For orders that are not completed the cookie is keep for two weeks allowing you to finish your order. Otherwise the cookie is removed. Credit card numbers are used for payment processing only and are not retained for other purposes.

Public Forums, Postings, Communications: We may, but are not obligated to, monitor or review any areas on the NaniPics site where users post communications or images, or transmit or communicate solely with each other, including but not limited to chat rooms, bulletin boards, or other user forums, and the content of any such communications. Any information disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security: The NaniPics site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All purchases are handled via a secure server to protect credit card information. You will notice a closed lock on any pages dealing with payment information.

Choice/Opt-Out. We provide users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from NaniPics via an unsubscribe mechanism in all e-mail communications.
